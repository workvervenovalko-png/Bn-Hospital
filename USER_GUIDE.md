# 🏥 BN Hospital Enterprise - User Manual & Operating Guide

Ye guide aapko batayegi ki **BN Hospital Management System** ko alag-alag departments kaise use karenge aur kiski kya power hai.

---

## 🔑 1. Login aur Credentials (ID/Password) Kaise Banenge?

BN Hospital ek **Private Enterprise** system hai, iska matlab har koi bahar se account nahi bana sakta.

*   **Admin Control:** Sirf Admin hi naye staff ya doctors ko system mein add kar sakta hai.
*   **Process:** 
    1. Admin **"Workforce"** section mein jayega.
    2. **"Onboard Staff"** par click karke details (Email, Name, Role) bharega.
    3. Ek baar Admin ne add kar diya, toh us staff ka account database mein ban jayega.
    4. Default password abhi system-generated hai (aap isse settings mein badal sakte hain).
*   **Approval:** Agar koi login kar bhi leta hai, toh jab tak Admin use **"Approve"** nahi karega, wo dashboard nahi dekh payega.

---

## 👑 2. Admin (Super Power)
Admin hospital ka boss hai. Uske paas ye powers hain:
*   **Sab Kuch Dekhna:** Admin har department ka data dekh sakta hai (Revenue, Patients, Staff).
*   **Staff Approval:** Naye employees ko approve ya block karna.
*   **Finance Reports:** Pure hospital ki kamayi (Revenue) ki Excel/CSV reports nikalna.
*   **Inventory Control:** Medicine stock check karna aur audit karna.

---

## 👩‍💼 3. Receptionist (Patient Entry)
Receptionist ka kaam patient ko register karna aur system mein entry dena hai.
*   **Kaise Karein?**
    1. Dashboard par **"Admission Portal"** ya sidebar mein **"Patients"** par jayein.
    2. Naye patient ki details (Name, Age, History) bharein.
    3. System apne aap ek **Unique Patient ID** (e.g., BN-1001) generate kar dega.
*   **Appointment:** Patient ko kis doctor ke paas bhejna hai, wo list se select karein.

---

## 👨‍⚕️ 4. Doctor (Clinical Work)
Doctor sirf clinical data aur patients ki history dekhte hain.
*   **Kaise Karein?**
    1. **"Patients"** list mein jayein aur us patient par click karein jiska checkup karna hai.
    2. **Consultation Form** khulega. Wahan bimari (Diagnosis) aur dawaiyan (Medicines) likhein.
    3. **Lab Recommendation:** Agar koi test chahiye, toh list mein likh dein.
    4. **Finalize:** Submit karte hi patient ki **PDF Prescription** download ho jayegi jo patient ko di ja sakti hai.

---

## 🧪 5. Lab Technician (Diagnostics)
Lab wale sirf wahi tests dekhte hain jo doctors ne prescribe kiye hain.
*   **Kaise Karein?**
    1. **"Lab Tests"** section mein jayein.
    2. Wahan saare **PENDING** tests ki list dikhegi.
    3. Test hone ke baad **"Upload Results"** par click karein aur findings likh dein.
    4. Report save hote hi doctor ko results dikhne lagenge.

---

## 💊 6. Pharmacy (Medicine & Inventory)
Pharmacy wale stock manage karte hain aur dawai dispense karte hain.
*   **Inventory Management:**
    1. **"Pharmacy"** section mein jayein.
    2. Agar kisi medicine ka stock kam hai (Orange/Red status), toh **"+" (Restock)** button daba kar stock badha dein.
    3. Nayi medicine add karni ho toh **"Add Medicine"** par click karein.
*   **Dispensing:** Doctor ki prescription dekh kar patient ko dawai dein.

---

## 💰 7. Billing Department (Finance)
Billing wale hospital ki fees aur medicine bills settle karte hain.
*   **Kaise Karein?**
    1. **"Billing"** section mein jayein.
    2. Patient ki ID search karein. Unka total bill apne aap dikh jayega.
    3. **Authorize Payment:** Paisa milne par "Mark as Paid" karein.
    4. **Tax Invoice:** Payment hone ke baad **Download Invoice** button se patient ko paki raseed (PDF) dein.

---

## 📊 8. Reports & Analytics (Final Audit)
Ye mostly Admin ya Manager use karenge.
*   **Excel Export:** Agar mahine ke end mein dekhna hai kitne patient aaye ya kitni dawai biki, toh **"Reports"** section mein jayein.
*   **Usage:** "Excel (.xlsx)" button dabayein aur poora data aapke computer mein spreadsheet ki tarah save ho jayega.
